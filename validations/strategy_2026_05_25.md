## Risk-cap analysis

Current settings:
- `MAX_TRADE_USD=5`, `MAX_TOTAL_LIVE_USD=100`, `MAX_OPEN_POSITIONS=10`

If we apply the backtest realized PnL linearly:
- $5  trade × 166 trades → +$126 (current backtest result)
- $10 trade × 166 → +$252
- $25 trade × 166 → +$630
- $50 trade × 166 → +$1,260

But `MAX_TOTAL_LIVE_USD=100` caps lifetime spend at ~20 trades worth. The 166 backtest trades would have hit the cap within hours; the rest never execute. **The lifetime cap is the binding constraint, not the per-trade size.**

To capture the projected PnL you'd need to:
1. Raise `MAX_TOTAL_LIVE_USD` enough to accommodate concurrent + cumulative spend (e.g., 10 concurrent × $5 = $50, but lifetime turnover of ~$800 over a week)
2. Make sure USDC wallet has at least the new per-trade cap value at all times (A2 balance gate will block otherwise)
